Homemade Pizza Recipe

November 24, 2024

Introduction

Making pizza at home is easier than you think! This recipe lets you customize toppings and enjoy fresh, hot pizza straight from your oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base or dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the pizza base, leaving a small border around the edges.
  3. Top with cheese, vegetables, and your favorite seasonings.
  4. Bake for 10–12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  5. Slice and enjoy your homemade pizza fresh from the oven!

Topping Ideas

Experiment with toppings like pepperoni, olives, pineapple, or extra cheese to make it your own!
