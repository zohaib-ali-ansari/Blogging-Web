Fresh and Healthy Salad Recipe

November 24, 2024

Introduction

Salads are the perfect blend of nutrition and flavor. Whether you're looking for a quick meal or a refreshing side, this vibrant salad is loaded with fresh veggies, protein, and a zesty dressing.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed greens (spinach, arugula, lettuce)
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 1/4 cup cucumber slices
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup croutons
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry the mixed greens thoroughly.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the greens, tomatoes, cucumbers, and carrots.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, honey, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  5. Top with croutons and serve immediately for maximum freshness.

Variations

Add grilled chicken, tofu, or nuts for extra protein. Swap lemon juice for balsamic vinegar for a tangy twist!
