Juicy Homemade Burger Recipe

November 24, 2024

Introduction

Who doesn’t love a juicy burger? Skip the fast food and try this easy recipe to create restaurant-quality burgers in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb ground beef (or chicken)
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 burger buns
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • Lettuce, tomato, and onion slices
  • Ketchup, mustard, or mayo

Instructions

  1. Mix ground beef with seasonings and form two patties.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook patties for 3–4 minutes per side.
  3. Place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking.
  4. Toast burger buns lightly in the skillet.
  5. Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, onion, patties, and your favorite sauces.
  6. Serve hot with fries or salad on the side!

Tips

Use fresh ground beef with a little fat content for juicy burgers. Add pickles or bacon for extra flavor!
