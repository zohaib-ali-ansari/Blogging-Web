The Best Chocolate Cake

November 20, 2024

Introduction

If you love chocolate, this rich, moist chocolate cake will be your go-to recipe for every special occasion! It’s easy to make and guaranteed to impress.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease two 9-inch cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, buttermilk, warm water, oil, and vanilla extract. Beat with an electric mixer until smooth.
  4. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans and bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  5. Cool the cakes completely before frosting with your favorite chocolate buttercream!

Frosting Suggestions

Pair this cake with a creamy chocolate ganache or a light cream cheese frosting for the perfect combination of flavors.
